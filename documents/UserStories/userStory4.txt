User Story: As a user, I should be able to see the local shows of the artists in my library as well as artists recommended 
for me.

Story description: 

Upon entering a correct city, the user will be able to see list of concerts based on their favorite artists on their Apple 
Music Library or Spotify library as well as a list of recommended concerts based on their favorites on their Apple Music 
library or Spotify library. If there are no concerts in their selected area for their favorite artists, the web application 
will only show recommended artists concerts.
